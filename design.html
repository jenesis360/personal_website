Design

I have been involved in many graphic design projects over the years, from print publicity to websites and branding. These days, I impart this experience into the slides for the many research talks I give.

Print

I have also been involved in several one-off publicity materials for the Oxford Undergraduate Physics Conference (OUPC), Turl Street Arts Festival (TSAF) and Durham's Young Theorist and Experimentalist Institute (YETI).
